How It Works
Setup guide and system architecture
🔧 Setup in 4 Steps
1
Add Faculty Members — Go to Faculty page and enter each faculty member's name, email, and department. These are the people who will receive emails.
2
Connect Google Sheet — Paste your sheet URL on the Dashboard. Your sheet should have columns: Title, Author, ISBN, Department, Accession No, Date Added. Books will auto-import.
3
Configure EmailJS — Sign up at emailjs.com (free), create a service linked to your Gmail/Outlook, create an email template, and paste your Service ID, Template ID, and Public Key in Settings.
4
Send! — Click "Send Notifications". The system groups books by department, matches them to faculty, fetches book covers via ISBN from Open Library, and sends beautiful HTML emails to each faculty group.
